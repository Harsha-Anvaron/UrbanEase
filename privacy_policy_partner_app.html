“Processing” means collection, storage, use, disclosure, transfer, or deletion of Personal Data.

  • Vehicle and Visitor Information: Vehicle registration number, check-in/check-out times, visitor phone number, and booking identifiers.

  • Device and Technical Information: Device model, operating system, app version, IP address, and activity logs.
